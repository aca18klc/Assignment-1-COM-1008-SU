Grill and Co

You can find the best wrap in Grill and Co. Their burrito is a must try! You can also get salad boxes here, perfect for lunch!

New Leaf

In New Leaf, you can make yourself take away salad or wrap. They have the freshest and healthiest ingredients. It also save you lots of time. It is located opposite SSiD on the ground floor.

Proper Pasty

Pasty lover? Proper Pasty will the perfect place for you. All their pasties are freshly hand made by a team of expert bakers. It is located opposite Santander on Level 3.

The (V)iew Deli

If you are a vegetarian, then The (V)iew Deli will the perfect place for you. Their food are not only for vegetarian-friendly but also gluten free. It is located on the Level 4.

Pearls

Pearls is a bubble tea shop. Bubble tea is a kind of drink origined from Taiwan and is famous in some Asia country. It is a mixture of tea and milk or other flavourings. Pearls is located on Level 3.
