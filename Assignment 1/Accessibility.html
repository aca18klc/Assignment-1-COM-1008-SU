Accessibility

In all the webpages, we have black background for the header, footer and menu. The content will be in white background instead. The purpose of this design is to make it easier for people to read and avoid confusion. It doesn't affect color blind people that much as well.

We apologise if the images cause any inconvenience to those with color blind. The images are only for reference purpose.

For further details, please go to the official website of Student Union.
